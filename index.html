audiotheme framework demo

The Audio Theme Framework plugin is built to work with any WordPress theme,providing the features needed for music-oriented website and allowing you to take you content with you anywhere you go - even when switching themes

In this demo, we've showcased how the Audio Theme Framework will look with the Twenty Fourteen theme and a little help with this child theme we've created. Take a look around at the functionality for displaying gigs and venues,discography (liner notes for the web), and videos.
